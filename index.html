The Problem

Thousands of kilos of crops in Mindanao spoil every year because farmers cannot find direct buyers. Middlemen set low farmgate prices, logistics are unreliable, and many farmers lack smartphone access.

Our Solution

AgriLink is a hybrid marketplace: a web dashboard for buyers and an SMS listing system for farmers who don’t have smartphones. Farmers post surplus in seconds and buyers discover local produce in real time.

  • Direct farmer-to-buyer matching
  • SMS listings for non-smartphone users
  • Price transparency and market insights
  • Logistics coordination and aggregation

How it works (quick)

  1. Farmer texts: "Tomato – 50 kg – Barangay X"
  2. AgriLink lists it on the marketplace
  3. Buyer views listing, places order
  4. Pickup arranged via cooperative or transport partner

Transactions are confirmed. Payments can be bank transfer, mobile money, or cash on delivery depending on agreement.
